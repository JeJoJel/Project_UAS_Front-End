Date :

We've been sharing candle making tutorials on this blog for nearly a decade, so we wanted to put together this beginner's guide to candle making with all our best tips and tricks, plus links to past projects you may want to try.

Making candles is an easy DIY project that anyone can do. It does require a few basic supplies, including a stovetop or something similar to melt the wax.
